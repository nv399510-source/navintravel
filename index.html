Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?

expand_more

You can cancel your booking through the 'My Bookings' section in your account or by calling our support line. Refunds are processed within 5-7 working days based on the cancellation policy.

Is there a luggage limit?

expand_more

Each passenger is allowed 15kg of luggage in the cargo hold and one small handbag inside the cabin. Additional charges may apply for oversized luggage.

Can I change my boarding point?

expand_more

Yes, boarding point changes are allowed up to 4 hours before departure through our customer support team or website dashboard.
